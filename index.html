Bangladesh launches New Satellite

Date:09-10-1975  Time: 10 AM

Bangladesh launched its first satellite, **Bangabandhu-1**, on May 12, 2018, using SpaceX’s Falcon 9 rocket. It provides communication, broadcasting, and internet services, making Bangladesh the 57th country with a satellite. Future plans include **Bangabandhu-2**, aimed at Earth observation and remote sensing to support agriculture, disaster management, and environmental monitoring.
